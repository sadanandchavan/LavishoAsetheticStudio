Medi Facial Treatment

Medical-grade facials, or medi-facials, bring benefits over more than that of your typical salon facial. These therapies have been designed to treat specific skin issues or problems and are suitable for almost all skin types. Medifacial and photofacial are both types of facial treatments that can help to improve the appearance of your skin. Medi-facials are performed at a dermatologist's clinic utilising a variety of instruments and procedures such as microcurrents, dermaplaning, photo rejuvenation, LED therapy, peels, microdermabrasion, and more.

They can help with a variety of skin conditions such as dull, dry, and itchy skin. They may recover the skin's younger-looking glowing and nourished appearance. They can be modified to address sun damage, pigmentation problems, and ageing indications.

Medical-grade facials known as medi facials thoroughly cleanse, exfoliate, and nourish the skin. The ingredients that are used in the treatment are completely skin-friendly and have been extensively tested on all skin types. They execute on the skin's deeper layers to promote the production of collagen, giving the skin a bright, vibrant look.

In this technique, we first cleanse the skin using non-irritating cleaners to take away any dust or makeup. We then use a specialised equipment to remove the dead skin cells from the epidermis. The skin will be hydrated in combination using a particular cosmeceutical, such as vitamin C or hyaluronic acid.

Benefits of a medi-facial

The advantages of a medi-facial are numerous. Spa facials typically don't penetrate the skin deeply enough to have an effective result. They lack substance. For effective and long-lasting results in medi-facials,equipment like ultrasonics and mesoporation can be combined with intraceuticals like vitamins, serums, chemical peels, and plant extracts.
